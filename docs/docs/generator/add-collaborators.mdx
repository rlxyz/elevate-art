# The Leading Art Generator

---

With [Elevate.Art](http://Elevate.Art) Art Generator, you can easily generate 10k NFT collections with no code. 

You will be able to generate your first collection in seconds once you have uploaded your image layers which is as simple as dropping in your images into the browser.

From there you will be able to adjust collection size, adjust rarity, create layer based and trait based rules and regenerate your collection in seconds.
