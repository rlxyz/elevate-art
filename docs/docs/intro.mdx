# The Leading Art Generator

---

### Welcome!

Thanks for choosing to work with Elevate Art to create your new generative Art and NFT project with us!

With our Art Generator you can easily generate mulitiple 1-20k collections with your image layers in (milli)seconds with no code.

Below we have provided a bunch of easy to use Guides to help you get started using the Art Generator, and if you have any troubles please don't hesitate to join our [Discord](https://discord.gg/) to ask questions and learn more about the community.

### Guides

- [Set Up Your Account:](../docs/account-setup) Create your team and project in a few simple steps.

- [Create Your First Project](../docs/generator/upload-art) Create your first Project by uploading your art image layers.

- [Curating Your Collection](../docs/generator/rarity-table) Refine your Collection by adjusting Rarity, Rules and more.

- [Export Your Collection](../docs/generator/export-art) Export your collection to IPFS and mint your NFTs.
